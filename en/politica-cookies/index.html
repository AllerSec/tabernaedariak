Cookie policy

Information about the technical storage this website uses and how to manage cookies in your browser.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites install on the user's device to store and retrieve information about their browsing. Alongside cookies there are other browser local storage technologies, such as sessionStorage and localStorage, which perform similar functions but are not sent to any server.

2. Storage this website uses

This website does not use analytics, advertising or tracking cookies. It only uses the browser's local technical storage, strictly necessary for its correct functioning:

  • sessionStorage (first-visit indicator): allows it to remember, only during the current browsing session, that the page's entry animation has already been shown. It is automatically deleted when the tab or browser is closed.
  • localStorage (cookie notice preference): stores the choice made in the cookie notice (accept or reject) so as not to show it again on later visits. It remains in the browser until the user deletes it.

This information is stored exclusively on the user's device, is not transmitted to our servers or to third parties and does not allow any person to be identified.

3. Third-party cookies: Google map

The contact page includes an embedded Google Maps map. When that map loads, Google may install its own third-party cookies over which Manuel Taberna S.L. has no control. You can consult information about Google's use of cookies on its official page: policies.google.com/technologies/cookies.

4. How to manage, block or delete cookies

Users can allow, block or delete the cookies and local storage installed on their device through their browser's settings. The official help pages of the most widely used browsers are listed below:

  • Google Chrome: support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
  • Mozilla Firefox: support.mozilla.org
  • Microsoft Edge: support.microsoft.com
  • Apple Safari: support.apple.com

Please note that blocking local technical storage may affect functions of this website, such as remembering your choice in the cookie notice.

5. Updates to this policy

This cookie policy may be updated if the website incorporates new storage technologies or third-party services in the future. The version in force will at all times be the one published on this page.

For any questions about this policy you can write to us at info@tabernaedariak.eus.
