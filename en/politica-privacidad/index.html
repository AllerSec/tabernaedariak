Privacy policy

How we process personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation and Spanish law.

This privacy policy is established in compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council, of 27 April 2016, on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data (GDPR), and with Spanish Organic Law 3/2018, of 5 December, on the Protection of Personal Data and guarantee of digital rights (LOPDGDD).

2. Data we process and how we obtain it

This website has no contact or registration forms, so browsing its pages does not involve the collection of personal data on our part.

The only personal data we process is that which the data subject voluntarily provides when contacting us by telephone or email: name, contact details, establishment details and the information included in their enquiry or order.

3. Purposes of processing

The data provided is processed for the following purposes:

  • To handle enquiries, requests for information and price-list requests received by telephone or email.
  • To manage the commercial relationship with customers: orders, deliveries, invoicing and after-sales service.

No profiling or automated decision-making is carried out with the data processed.

4. Legal basis

The legal basis for processing is:

  • The data subject's consent (Article 6.1.a GDPR), given when voluntarily contacting us to make an enquiry or request information.
  • The performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (Article 6.1.b GDPR), where processing is necessary to manage the commercial relationship with customers.

5. Retention period

Data will be kept for as long as the commercial relationship is maintained or for as long as necessary to handle the enquiry made and, once that relationship has ended, for the periods legally required to address any potential liabilities, in particular those provided for in commercial, tax and accounting regulations.

6. Recipients

Personal data is not disclosed to third parties, except by legal obligation (for example, to the tax authorities or to judges and courts where required by applicable law).

No international data transfers are carried out.

7. Rights of data subjects

Anyone has the right to obtain confirmation as to whether Manuel Taberna S.L. is processing personal data concerning them. Specifically, data subjects may exercise the following rights:

  • Access: to know what data of theirs we are processing.
  • Rectification: to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: to request the deletion of their data when, among other reasons, it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Objection: to object to the processing of their data on grounds relating to their particular situation.
  • Portability: to receive their data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format, or to request its transmission to another controller.
  • Restriction of processing: to request that the processing of their data be restricted in the cases provided for by law.

These rights may be exercised by sending a request, accompanied by a copy of an identity document, by email to info@tabernaedariak.eus or by post to Manuel Taberna S.L., Bidasoa karrika 76, 31780 Bera (Navarre).

Likewise, if the data subject considers that the processing of their data infringes data protection regulations, they have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD), located at C/ Jorge Juan 6, 28001 Madrid, through its website www.aepd.es.

8. Data security

Manuel Taberna S.L. applies appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, in accordance with Article 32 GDPR, in order to protect personal data against loss, alteration, unauthorised processing or access.

9. Updates to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated to adapt to regulatory changes or changes in the company's activity. The version in force will at all times be the one published on this page.
