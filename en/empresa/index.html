The house Etxea

More than 75 years serving the region. Since 1949, the drinks for the bars, restaurants and shops of Bortziriak, Malerreka and Baztán have left our warehouse in Bera.

History · Historia

From 1949 to today, never letting go of the wheel

Manuel Taberna began delivering drinks across the region in 1949, when orders were written down by hand and the roads were different. In 1988 the business was incorporated as Manuel Taberna S.L., but the essence hasn't changed: a family business from Bera that knows every customer by name.

Today we are a team of around ten people still doing the same as back then, only with more product families, more official brands and the same drive: that your business never runs short of anything.

Customers · Bezeroak

Who we serve

We work for those who feed and serve drinks to the region. If your business serves a coffee, a pintxo or a full table, we are your supplier.

  • Bars and cafés — from the midday draught to the morning coffee.
  • Restaurants — wine list, waters, coffees and everything that crosses the table.
  • Gastronomic societies — the pantry always full for dinners and celebrations.
  • Shops and retailers — weekly restocking without leaving the counter.
Warehouse and delivery · Biltegia eta banaketa

From Agerra to your door, every week

Our drivers travel the whole region taking our products to your bars, restaurants and shops. Even so, you can also come and see us if you need anything: the doors are open.

The warehouse is in the Agerra neighbourhood of Bera, open Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 15:00. From there the weekly delivery sets out to every town in Bortziriak —Bera, Lesaka, Etxalar, Arantza and Igantzi—, Malerreka and the Baztán valley.
