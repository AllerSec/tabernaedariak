The house · Etxea

Three quarters of a century serving the region

Manuel Taberna S.L. is a family business from Bera devoted to wholesale beverage distribution since 1949. We know every bar, every restaurant and every shop in the area, because we have been coming through their doors for generations.

  • Weekly visit and delivery to every town in Bortziriak, Malerreka and Baztán.
  • Direct service at the Agerra warehouse in Bera: come whenever you need to, the doors are open.
  • Official distributors of Heineken, Schweppes, Insalus, Lacturale, Zapiain, Codorníu, Pago and Ur Daira.

Delivery area

Wherever you are, we get there

Weekly delivery to every town in these three districts of northern Navarre.

Bortziriak · Cinco Villas

Bera, Lesaka, Etxalar, Arantza and Igantzi. Our home: the warehouse is here and it all began here in 1949.

Malerreka

Doneztebe/Santesteban and every town in the valley. Weekly visits to bars, societies and shops.

Baztán

Elizondo and the whole Baztán valley, from Oronoz to Amaiur. Reliable delivery every week, including in high season.

Frequently asked questions

What people usually ask us

Which areas do you deliver to?

Every week we visit and deliver to all the towns in Bortziriak (Bera, Lesaka, Etxalar, Arantza and Igantzi), Malerreka and the Baztán valley.

What products do you distribute?

All kinds of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages — wines, beers, whiskies, liqueurs, soft drinks, cavas, cider and txakoli — plus coffee, milk, water, oil, preserves, juices, glassware and miniatures. 18 product families in total.

Can I buy directly at the warehouse?

Yes. We are at Bidasoa karrika 76, in Bera, Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 15:00. Whatever you need, the doors are open.

How do I place an order?

Call us on 948 631 027 or 670 598 560, email us at info@tabernaedariak.eus, or tell your driver during the weekly visit.
